What is exactly Floriography?
Florigraphy is the symbolic language of flowers, which can be used in many situations.
As you can see, Shakespeare wrote about these “talking flowers”, and in the Victorian era
it became the most important part of the life that you needed to keep a secret...
Sending messages witch carried the meaning of love and care for each other and it
cannot be said out loud because of the social situations.
